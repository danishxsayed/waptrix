The WhatsApp Business API (now called WhatsApp Business Platform) is Meta's official solution for medium and large businesses to send and receive WhatsApp messages at scale. Unlike the regular WhatsApp Business app (which is limited to one user and 256 contacts per broadcast), the API supports unlimited contacts, bulk campaigns, CRM integrations, and team inboxes. Waptrix is built on top of this API, so everything you do through Waptrix is 100% official and compliant with Meta's policies.
